Everything Skillet

Description

The Everything Skillet is an easy to make breakfast the packs all the calories you'll need to tackle the day! Its got potatoes, meats, veggies, and eggs! What more does a person need?

Run down to the store and get this list of ingredients and get to cookin'!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • hash browns
  • chopped onion
  • chopped green and red peppers
  • chopped ham
  • chopped bacon
  • ground breakfast sausage
  • shredded cheese
  • milk

Directions

  1. Crack the desired amount of eggs into a bowl. Add a splash of milk. Beat eggs with wisk or fork till color is even and set to side.
  2. In a skillet add 2 tablespoons of oil and bring to medium-high heat. Add hashbrowns and cook till golden brown.
  3. In a second non-stick skillet add tablespoon of oil and chopped ham sausage and bacon. Cook till done.
  4. Add the the onions and peppers and sautee for 4 minutes.
  5. Add the eggs and mix everything up in single non-stick pan. Stir occasionally. Done when eggs are fluffly and no longer 'wet' looking.
  6. Add the egg, meat, and veggie mix to the top of the hashbrowns. Top with shredded cheese.
  7. EAT UP!
