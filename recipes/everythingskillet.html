Everything Skillet

Description

The Everything Skillet is an easy to make breakfast the packs all the calories you'll need to tackle the day! Its got potatoes, meats, veggies, and eggs! What more does a person need?

Run down to the store and get this list of ingredients and get to cookin'!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • hash browns
  • chopped onion
  • chopped green and red peppers
  • chopped ham
  • chopped bacon
  • ground breakfast sausage
  • shredded cheese
  • milk
