Taco In A Bag

Description

Tacos in a Bag or Walkie Tacos are a fun twist on everyones favorite Tex-Mex dish! Its as simple as making tacos like you normally would but instead of using a shell you add the toppings right into your favortie bag of Doritos!

So run to the grocery store and pick up these ingredents and get ready to make your families NEW FAVORITE!

Ingredients

  • bags of Doriots
  • 1 tomatoe
  • 1 onion
  • 1lbs ground beef or chicken breasts
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • sour cream
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 bag shredded cheese
  • taco sauce

Direction

  1. Brown ground beef in a large skillet on medium to medium-high setting for 5-8 minutes.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water and let simmer for 8 minutes.
  3. Chop onmion and tomatoe up and put off to the side.
  4. Take bag of Doritos and place on its side and cut the long way. Crush chips up a bit.
  5. Add a spoon full of meat to your bag of Doritos.
  6. Add cheese next.
  7. Next you will add the onion followed by the lettuce and topped off with the tomatoe.
  8. Next add taco sauce and sour cream.
  9. DIG IN!
