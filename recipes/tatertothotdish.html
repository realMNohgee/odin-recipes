Tater-Tot Hotdish

Description

Tater-Tot Hotdish is an easy crowd pleaser that can be prepafred in 15 minutes and ready to eat in under an hour! This dish is a midwest staple, popular in Minnesota, Wisconsin, North Dakota, South Dakota, and parts of Montana.

The dish is easily made your own by adding an assortment of veggies and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 Can Cream of Mushroom
  • 1 Can Corn
  • 1 Bag of Tater-Tots

Steps

  1. Put ground beef into skillet and brown meat for 5-10 minutes. Strtain and put in mixing bowl.
  2. Add one can of cream of mushroom to mixing bowl with browned meat and mix well.
  3. In a large non-stick cake pan evenly spread mixed meat and cream of mushroom.
  4. Open can of corn and strain water out. Spread out over top of meat in pan.
  5. Spread Tater-Tots evenly across the top of corn and meat mix. Be sure to cover whole pan.
  6. Cover with tin foil and set on middle rack of oven pre-heated to 350 degrees.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and let sit for 5-10 minutes. Add cheese to top if desired.
  9. Eat up!
